Project One.

"Company Home Page"

Scourcing images and designing layout of company homepage by writing own HTML and CSS, to have the scale of the elements respond to resizing the browser using "flex" css rules.

I was quite impressed with how relitively simple the layouts can scale down, - sure its a loose cosmetic look but the image and text elements remained clear and in structure.

Being one of the first "off platform" projects it was really refreshing to break from the typical lesson layout, to have choces over design and put the rules into real practice gave me confidence.
